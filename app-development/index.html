Apps that feel considered and ship on time.

From sketch to store

A good app is not a pile of features. It is a small number of things done so well that people come back. We build for that: a clear core, a considered interface, and the engineering discipline to make it fast and stable. AI helps us move quickly through the parts that used to take weeks, so more time goes into the parts that make an app feel finished.

What we deliver

  • iOS, Android, and web. Native or cross-platform, chosen for your users and roadmap, not our habits.
  • Product design that earns loyalty. Interfaces that are obvious to use and quietly enjoyable to return to.
  • Store submission and compliance. App Store and Google Play review handled end to end, including privacy and support pages, so it passes the first time.
  • Back-end and APIs. The infrastructure that makes the app work, built to scale sensibly.
  • Maintenance and monitoring. We keep it healthy, or hand it over fully documented. Your call.

Built with AI, finished by people

We use AI to accelerate scaffolding, testing, and iteration. We do not ship whatever it produces. Every screen, flow, and line that reaches your users passes through senior review. Speed is only useful if the result is something you are proud to put your name on.

The goal is not an app that launches. It is an app that lasts.

What you can count on

Direct access to the builders. A fixed scope and timeline. Full ownership of the code and every account. If the first milestone does not land, you do not pay for it.

Common questions

Native or cross-platform?

Whichever serves the product. We choose based on your users, budget, and roadmap, and we tell you the trade-off honestly.

Do you handle App Store submission?

Yes, end to end: App Store and Google Play submission, review, and compliance, including privacy and support requirements.

What happens after launch?

We maintain and improve it, or hand it over fully documented so your team owns it. You decide.
